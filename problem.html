What is Package Theft

Package theft is a big deal and it can cause millions to lose their money. The thiefs, commonly refered to as "Porch Pirates" are very malignant and are very rarely caught. Only 10% of all package theft cases are brougt to justice, which is astonishing thinking that over 100 million people suffer from this. In the U.S, there are 327 milllion people, and 1/3 of the people suffer from package thert. That is unheard of.There are many devices that people use to prevent this sort of theft to happen, but none of them seem to be useful because the numbers just keep rising.
