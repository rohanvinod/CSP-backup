Package Theft Prevention System

Our device is called the Package Theft Prevention System, or PTPS for short. Basically, this device will alert you when there is someone close by. This device uses a motion sensor, which is coded from the ARDUINO programming language. The device will also be connected to the PTPS app which can then send you notifications easier and will allow you to customize the system to your needs and wants. There are many other ways of keeping your packages secure, nut our device is more capable of keeping the users package safe. The security Camera is a good way to keep the package safe, but sometimes, they require a wifi connection to work, but our device can work without a wifi connection. Also, Amazon Key, is a service which grants strangers access to your house so that they can place packages inside your house. This is dangerous because it creates the risk of the stranger stealing things inside your house, and that is what we are trying to prevent with our device. We built the whole system from an arduino kit.The system consists of:
  • Arduino Uno Board
  • Small Breadboard
  • PIR Motion Sensor
  • Female to Female Wires
  • Female to Male Wires
  • Male to Male Wires
  • Transistors
  • Macbook/Laptop
