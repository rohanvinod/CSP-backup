What Is Arduino?

Adrunio is an open-sourced elctronic platform that reads and follows inputs based on the user's desire. Ardunio can do simple tasks such as activating a motor or turing a light switch on and off. You set these instructions on to the arduino by using the arduino programming language. The purpose of the arduino in the use of arduino in our project is too be a sensor in our product. The arduino commands will be altered so that it will sense a figure approaching it and then alert the buyer or the resident of the house hold that their package is at a risk of being stolen.
